# Legal Positioning

## Service Classification

AllVision is an informational service.
It provides price-comparison and sourcing reports about eyeglasses lenses from suppliers in the EU and Switzerland.

## Explicit Non-Commercial Scope

AllVision does not sell lenses.
AllVision does not broker, mediate, or execute purchases.
AllVision is not a marketplace and is not a seller of medical devices.

## Medical Disclaimer

AllVision does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or prescription interpretation.
Users must consult qualified eye-care professionals for medical guidance.

## Geographic Scope

Service scope is limited to lens sourcing information within EU member states and Switzerland.

## Required User-Facing Disclaimer (Baseline Text)

"AllVision is an informational report service only. We do not sell lenses, broker transactions, or provide medical advice."

## Forbidden Claims

Do not claim or imply:

- clinical effectiveness of lenses,
- health outcomes,
- guaranteed savings,
- purchase execution by AllVision.

## Operational Guardrails

- UI copy must distinguish report purchase from lens purchase.
- Invoices/receipts describe the product as "information report service".
- Support scripts must avoid medical recommendations.
