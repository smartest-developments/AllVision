# Legal Positioning

## Service Classification

AllVision is an informational service.
The product sold is an offshore sourcing report containing comparative pricing and sourcing information for eyeglasses lenses in the EU + Switzerland.

## Explicit Scope and Exclusions

AllVision does not sell lenses or any medical devices.
AllVision does not broker, mediate, arrange, or execute purchase transactions.
AllVision does not represent suppliers as an agent.

## Medical Disclaimer

AllVision does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or prescription determination.
Prescription-related data is processed only to produce comparative sourcing information.
Users must consult qualified eye-care professionals for medical decisions.

## User Responsibility

Users remain solely responsible for deciding whether, where, and how to purchase lenses.
AllVision provides comparative information only and does not guarantee suitability, outcomes, or savings.

## Liability Boundaries

- Report content is informational and based on available sources at preparation time.
- Prices, availability, and supplier terms may change after report issuance.
- AllVision is not liable for third-party supplier performance, product defects, or transaction disputes.

## Geographic Scope

Service scope is limited to sourcing information within EU member states and Switzerland.

## Required Baseline Disclaimer Text

"AllVision provides informational offshore sourcing reports only. We do not sell medical devices, broker transactions, or provide medical advice. Purchase decisions are your responsibility."

## Forbidden Claims

Do not claim or imply:

- guaranteed savings,
- medical efficacy or health outcomes,
- purchase execution by AllVision,
- endorsement of any supplier as medically superior.
